Pi Day of the Century

Pac-Math T-Shirt Fundraiser

Great Northern Printing discounted our custom T-shirts so we could sell them in a fundraiser. Funds went toward tools, the difference in T-shirt costs, custom plaques, the Unveiling Ceremony, and other materials. We met our goal of $450 by Pi Day, 3/14/15!

Donation Speech

To the Mathematics Department of SUNY Potsdam
